Amethyst - UBC Duplicate Site

For this project the task was to rebuild the amethyst info page in Wikipedia and use a Canadian Univeristy/College website to model the new website. The website I chose to model and rebuild the HTML5/CSS3/JS front-end from scratch with the Wikipedia article content, from one of the Canadian public universities/colleges was ‘The University of British Columbia’ also known as ‘UBC’. For the new information architecture, I created a new global navigation called about. This new global navigation has structure, hue and tone, and synthetic amethyst as its local navigation. I decided to put structure, together with hue and tone as the hue and tone can be considered another property of amethyst therefore, it would be appropriate to put it under the same global navigation. Synthetic amethyst is also a part of the structure and property which is why I put this as a local navigation (along with the other two) under the same global navigation (about).
